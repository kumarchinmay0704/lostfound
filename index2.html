AIM

Introducing our initiative: a Lost and Found website by BMSCE students, aimed at preventing valuable losses on campus.
Divided into Lost section and the other is the Found section. Users report items lost or found within the college/hostel premises. Details like time, place, and attributes are required.
False claims may result in penalties, as all claims are visible. Strengthening community bonds is our goal.
